I am a post-doctoral research associate at Purdue University working with Prof. Suresh Jagannathan.

My research interests are in Program Analysis, Formal Verification and Programming Languages. Recently, I have been working on developing automated verification techniques for programs running on distributed systems under weak consistency.

I was a PhD student at Indian Institute of Science under the guidance of Prof. YN Srikant, where I was supported by Microsoft Research India PhD Fellowship. During my PhD, I worked on the problem of statically estimating the impact of hardware caches on Worst Case Execution Time (WCET) of programs.
